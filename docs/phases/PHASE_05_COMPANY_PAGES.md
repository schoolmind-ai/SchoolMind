# Phase 05 — Official Company Page System

## Goal
Turn the public website into a structured company site, not a loose collection of marketing pages.

## Implemented
- Added `/product` as the platform overview page.
- Added `/features` as the product capability catalog.
- Added `/human-review` to explain the human-supervised operating model.
- Added `/compliance` as a truthful readiness overview without fake compliance badges.
- Added `/cookies` with a conservative school-safe cookie posture.
- Added `/request-demo` and `/contact-sales` for sales/demo routing.
- Added new endpoints to the sitemap.
- Expanded navigation/footer links so Product, Trust, and Business pages are discoverable.
- Added a dedicated company-site audit script.

## Guardrails
- No medical diagnosis claims.
- No fake customer or compliance claims.
- No invasive analytics or advertising cookies by default.
- Real production still requires legal review, payment provider, email provider, and production database.
